ABOUT

CMR HACKFEST 1.0 is a 36 Hours National-level Hackathon (Offline) hosted by CMR College of Engineering & Technology (UGC Autonomous Institution), Hyderabad, Telangana.

Education, Research and Innovation go hand-in-hand and with the intersection of disciplines, new ideas emerge and impulse innovation.

CMRCET is known as the top-notch Institution for its internationally renowned quality in Multidisciplinary Innovation.

CMR HACKFEST 1.0 is an exhilarating and innovative Hackathon and Project Competition that aims to bring together bright minds and creative thinkers from various institutions to collaborate, ideate and innovate in the realm of technology and beyond. It is an excellent opportunity for students to showcase their skills, network with peers and gain valuable experience in problem-solving.
